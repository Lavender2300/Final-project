~I'm Willow Raine~
I was born and raised in the Sierra Mountain Range. Since I was a little girl I've always wanted to help others. Growing up, I have seen so many people look for ways to relax and get a break from the stresses in their lives. I found massage therapy to be a really good way to help those relax in a holistic and therapeutic way. Living in a mountain community has influenced me in focusing on bringing my clients a safe space in a natural and relaxed environment. I am also a 2021 graduate of The National Holistic Institute, A College of Massage Therapy. If you'd like more information on my approach to massage therapy, please contact me. I look forward to hearing from you!
